Who we are

A solution provider, not just an event company

International Business Conferences (IBC) is one of India's leading conference, training, and events companies. For over 25 years we've specialised in planning and delivering conferences, awards, summits, workshops, in-house programs, and webinars.

We see ourselves as a solution provider — giving client organisations the technical expertise and insight they need to grow, stay compliant, and stay competitive, backed by deep industry relationships and an edge in adapting to the latest technology and regulation.

Our journey

25 years in the making

Tracing our growth from a focused training initiative to India's leading industry knowledge and B2B conference platform since 2001.

2001

IBC Founded

IBC founded in Mumbai by Nitin Vinod Shah, with a clear focus on corporate training and industry learning.

2000s

PSU & Corporate Focus

Digital Integration
