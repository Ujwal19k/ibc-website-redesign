Who we are

A solution provider, not just an event company

International Business Conferences (IBC) is one of India's leading conference, training, and events companies. For over 21 years we've specialised in planning and delivering conferences, awards, summits, workshops, in-house programs, and webinars.

We see ourselves as a solution provider — giving client organisations the technical expertise and insight they need to grow, stay compliant, and stay competitive, backed by deep industry relationships and an edge in adapting to the latest technology and regulation.

Our journey

21 years in the making

Tracing our growth from a focused training initiative to India's leading industry knowledge and B2B conference platform since 2001.

2001

IBC Founded

IBC founded in Mumbai by Nitin Vinod Shah, with a clear focus on corporate training and industry learning.

2000s

PSU & Corporate Focus

Establishes its authority across key PSU and enterprise sectors (Oil & Gas, Power, Human Resources, Safety).

2019

Digital Integration

Expands research capabilities and digital formats, transitioning programs for remote & hybrid learning options.

2022

Trendsetter Award

Receives the prestigious Trendsetter Award for excellence in corporate training & conferences, honoured by the Governor of Maharashtra.

2025+

Branded Conferences

Launches major sector summits: PeopleTech, Manufacturing 5.0, Safe India Conclave, and the OHS&E Awards.
