Overview

About the event

The PeopleTech Summit & Awards returns for its 2nd edition to examine how technology is reshaping the work of HR and People teams — from AI in hiring to skills, culture, and the employee experience.

Over one day in Pune, 30 senior People & Talent leaders share what's actually working inside their organisations. The programme pairs keynote perspective with practitioner panels, and closes with an awards ceremony recognising the year's most effective HR-tech initiatives.

Learn from leaders

CHROs and People heads from HUL, Tata, ITC and 25+ more, on the record.

Real use cases

Case studies of HR-tech deployments — not vendor pitches or theory.

Next-gen tools

A first look at the platforms changing hiring, L&D and engagement.

Celebrate excellence

An awards ceremony recognising standout HR-technology work across India.

  • CHROs, VPs & Heads of HR
  • Talent Acquisition & L&D leaders
